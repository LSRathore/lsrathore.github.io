About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Emory University. My work focuses on understanding how human activities and environmental changes affect water resources and food systems. I use geospatial data, process-based modeling, statistical approaches, and machine learning to analyze interactions between climate, agriculture, and water security for sustainable solutions.

Education

  • PhD, University of Alabama, 2024
  • BTech & MTech, Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur, 2018

Research Interests

  • Sustainable agriculture transition
  • Food-water security
  • Human-environmental interaction
  • Process-based modeling
  • Machine learning and data-driven environmental science
