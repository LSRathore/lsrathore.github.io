Lokendra Singh Rathore

Postdoctoral Research Fellow, Emory University

I research how human activities and environmental changes influence food and water systems. My work leverages geospatial data, statistical approaches, process-based models, and machine learning to analyze interactions between climate, management practices, conservation programs and policies, infrastructure, and socio-economic factors, with the goal of improving the sustainability and resilience of agricultural systems.
